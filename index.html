Projects

Flourish

Flourish is a motivational productivity app developed for my senior experience course, written in Kotlin and designed to make achieving goals both rewarding and enjoyable. The core concept revolves around a virtual plant that thrives with your productivity. As you complete tasks and goals, the plant receives care—watering, sunlight, and more. If you fall off track, the plant may wilt, but it can always be revived with renewed effort. This feature is intended to offer gentle encouragement without causing guilt, providing a calming and inviting user experience.

The app also includes a calendar that integrates with your task and goal lists. This allows you to plan your days and months or set deadlines as needed. A unique "shop" feature lets users spend virtual coins earned through daily challenges or consistent care of their plants. Here, you can purchase new plants, pots, pot skins, and even greenhouse environments, adding a fun and personal touch to the experience.

While the project's ambition was to create a comprehensive motivational tool, we faced time constraints with only one semester to develop it. As a result, some planned features, such as full calendar functionality and the complete range of virtual rewards, were not fully realized. Nonetheless, the project successfully showcased the concept of blending productivity with motivation in a visually engaging and supportive way.
