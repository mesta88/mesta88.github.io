Projects

Flourish

Dive into Flourish, a unique productivity app where your achievements grow a virtual garden of success! Developed using Kotlin for my senior experience course, Flourish transforms everyday tasks into a journey of growth and motivation. The concept is simple yet powerful: your virtual plant thrives on the care you provide by completing your tasks and goals. Each accomplishment waters, nourishes, or provides sunlight to your plant, helping it flourish into a beautiful and vibrant entity.

What sets Flourish apart is its emphasis on positive reinforcement and self-compassion. Instead of guilt-tripping you for falling behind, the app gently encourages you to get back on track whenever your plant wilts. Witnessing your plant’s revival and growth can be incredibly motivating, turning productivity into a rewarding and visually satisfying experience.

In addition to plant care, Flourish features a practical calendar that integrates seamlessly with your tasks and goals. It helps you plan your days or set deadlines, keeping you organized and on top of your schedule. And for a touch of fun and personalization, the app includes a "shop" where you can spend virtual coins—earned through daily challenges or consistent care—to purchase new plants, decorative pots, pot skins, and even greenhouse environments.

Another key feature is the built-in Journal, which serves as a handy text editor for jotting down thoughts, ideas, or reflections. This addition complements the productivity-focused elements of the app by providing a space for users to capture and organize their ideas effortlessly.

Working on Flourish was a significant milestone in my journey as it was one of the rare opportunities where my team and I had complete creative freedom. Unlike traditional assignments with strict guidelines, we were encouraged to bring our vision to life. This freedom allowed us to explore innovative ideas and create something truly meaningful, reflecting our passion and creativity.

Although we faced time constraints with just one semester to develop the app, and some features like the full calendar functionality and additional virtual rewards were not fully implemented, Flourish remains a proud accomplishment. It represents a blend of creativity, motivation, and technical skill, and it showcases how meaningful and enjoyable productivity can be.
