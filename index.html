About Me

Hi there! I’m Megan, a 33-year-old tech enthusiast with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science. My journey began in North Dakota and led me to Denver, CO, in 2012. After exploring various roles, I discovered my passion for software development and returned to school to pursue it.

My background includes customer support experience at a startup VoIP company, where I developed strong problem-solving skills and a deep understanding of user needs in a SaaS environment. I’m excited to blend these skills with my technical expertise to create innovative solutions.

Here are a few projects I’m particularly proud of:

  • Flourish: A mobile app designed to support mental health, built with Kotlin in Android Studio.
  • StreamStamp: A Chrome extension developed through a freelance project, showcasing my ability to create practical, user-friendly tools.
  • MegaBot: An interactive bot I created for various server functions, demonstrating my skills in building engaging digital experiences.

I believe in being open about my journey, including my GPA, to give you a fuller picture of my academic path and the hurdles I’ve overcome. My cumulative GPA of 2.71 is a testament to a period of significant struggle and growth. Early in my studies, I faced severe test anxiety that deeply impacted my performance. It was a tough time, and I struggled to keep up. Determined to turn things around, I reached out for help and worked hard to develop strategies to manage my anxiety. My final two semesters, which included some of the most challenging senior and upper-level courses, were where I truly began to see progress. Despite the difficulties, I managed to achieve GPAs of 3.0 and 3.5, reflecting not just my academic improvement, but also my perseverance and growth under pressure.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. I’m excited to apply my blend of technical skills and customer-focused experience to new opportunities.
